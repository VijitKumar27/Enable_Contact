Our Upcycling program

Our mission is also to increase awareness about the benefits of upcycling, especially targeting the young, who form the future of tomorrow.

We convert old uniforms to cloth bags. There are three places where you can help us out!

  • Schools/Individuals

    • You can partner as schools willing to give us your old uniforms.

  • Upcyclers

    • You can be the ones making the cloth bags from old uniforms.

  • Shopkeepers

    • You can buy these bags.
